Welcome!

 

Basic information

  • In this task, we need your help in comparing politicians based on various characteristics.
  • Also, we would like to get some basic information about yourself (gender, decade of birth, education degree, interest in politics, and political affiliation) to put your work in context.
  • Data from this research will be reported only as an aggregate. Your responses will not allow to identify you.
  • Completing all four tasks should take you 5-10 minutes.
  • You are allowed to participate in this HIT more than once. However, please do not participate more often than three times. You will not get compensated for your 4th, 5th etc. HIT.

 

Instructions

  • Please use the tabs on the side to navigate through all the tasks of this HIT.
  • Your data will not be lost when you switch between tasks.
  • Once you have completed all tasks, please submit your work by clicking on one of the Submit buttons.
  • Important: You will only get fully compensated for your work if you complete all tasks.

 

Contact

  • If you have any questions regarding the use of your work in this research, please do not hesitate to contact the principle investigator: Dr. Simon Munzert, Hertie School of Governance, Berlin, munzert@hertie-school.org.
 

Thank you very much for contributing to our research!
